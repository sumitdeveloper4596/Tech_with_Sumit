Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 25 November 2025

Tech With Sumit (TWS) ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have in connection with our mobile application Tech With Sumit (TWS).

1. Information We Collect

Currently the app does not require user registration and does not intentionally collect personal identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number. However, the app may automatically collect the following non-personal data:

  • Device information (device model, OS version, screen resolution)
  • App usage data (which screens are viewed, time spent in app)
  • Network and connectivity status
  • Error logs and crash reports (for debugging and improving the app)

These data are collected to analyze usage and improve app stability and performance.

2. Third-Party Content & Services

The app currently displays educational content using WebView and embedded YouTube videos. These are provided by third parties and are subject to their own privacy policies.

Notably, when you watch YouTube videos, Google may collect usage data according to Google's policies. We do not control or store data collected by YouTube/Google.

Advertising (Third-Party Ads Inside Websites)

The app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads are served by Google and may use cookies or device identifiers to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. Additionally, some third-party websites opened inside the app’s WebView may show their own advertisements, which are controlled and served by the respective website owners, not by us.

We do not:

  • control these ads
  • collect any advertising-related data

Any data collected by those websites or their advertisers is governed by their own privacy policies.

3. Downloadable Study Material

The app includes downloadable study materials such as PDFs. Downloaded files are saved only within the app's internal storage, meaning:

  • No external storage or file manager access is required
  • No storage permission is requested
  • Users cannot access or modify these files outside the app

We do not collect, upload, or share any downloaded files. These downloads are used solely to provide offline study content within the app.

4. Future Features (Premium Content & Accounts)

In the future we may add features such as paid/premium courses or optional user accounts. If we introduce any features that collect personal information, this Privacy Policy will be updated and users will be informed before any personal data is collected.

If accounts are introduced, we will clearly state what information is required (for example email for login) and how it will be used.

5. How We Use Collected Information

  • To diagnose and fix crashes and bugs
  • To provide and maintain the app experience

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may also use analytics tools to better understand user behavior. Analytics data will be aggregated and anonymized where possible.
